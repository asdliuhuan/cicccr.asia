About Our Company

Trusted California Licensed Contractor (CSLB) — Full-service residential construction and general contracting.

Excellence in Residential Building & General Contracting

Established on April 6, 2023, Homes By Platinum Inc. has rapidly earned recognition for delivering premium custom homes, modular construction, and comprehensive residential contracting services. Based in Yucca Valley, California, we hold a valid license with the California Contractors State License Board (CSLB).

We manage full home construction projects — from sitework and framing to final finishes. Our integrated approach ensures on-time delivery, superior craftsmanship, and transparent communication. We serve homeowners, developers, and investors throughout Yucca Valley, Joshua Tree, Palm Springs, and surrounding high-desert communities.

Our Mission

To redefine residential construction through integrity, precision, and innovation — building dream homes that stand the test of time while exceeding client expectations.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted general building contractor in Southern California, known for excellence in custom builds, modular solutions, and community-focused developments.
